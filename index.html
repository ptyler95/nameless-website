The Challenge: AI Visibility

The question we hear most: "My customers are using ChatGPT to explore and make their buying decisions. How can I make sure I show up?"

Traditional SEO isn't enough anymore. AI models don't just look for keywords ‚Äì they need to understand the context and relationships between your content, products, and brand. This is where structured data becomes essential.

What is Structured Data?

Structured data is machine-readable information embedded directly into your website's HTML. It provides explicit context about your brand, products, and content that AI models and search engines can easily understand and use to generate rich, relevant results.

Why Structured Data Works for AI

LLMs and AI search tools rank information based on context and understanding, not just keyword density. Structured data acts as a direct line of communication with these systems, telling them exactly:
