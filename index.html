The question we hear most: "My customers are using ChatGPT to explore and make their buying decisions. How can I make sure I show up?"

What is Structured Data?

Structured data is machine-readable information embedded directly into your website's HTML. It provides explicit context about your brand, products, and content that AI models and search engines can easily understand and use to generate rich, relevant results.

Why Structured Data Works for AI

LLMs and AI search tools rank information based on context and understanding, not just keyword density. Structured data acts as a direct line of communication with these systems, telling them exactly:

Our Implementation Process

We follow a proven methodology to ensure your structured data is implemented correctly and effectively:

1. Audit & Analysis

We analyze your current website structure and identify opportunities for structured data implementation.

2. Schema Design

We create comprehensive schema markup tailored to your business, including Organization, Product, Service, and LocalBusiness schemas as appropriate.

3. Implementation

We embed the structured data directly into your website's HTML, ensuring it's properly formatted and validated.

4. Testing & Validation

We use Google's Rich Results Test and Schema.org validators to ensure everything is working correctly.

5. Monitoring & Optimization

We track your AI visibility and search performance, making adjustments as needed to maximize results.
